Part B- Prevention checklist
1.Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and avoid reusing them
2. Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts such as email and banking apps 
3. Always verify the authenticity of links, messages, and websites before interacting with them 
4. Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information on public platforms 
5. Do not approve any unknown payment or collect requests on UPI apps 
6. Never scan QR codes sent by unknown individuals,
7. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for unusual activity
8. Be cautious of calls or messages claiming to be from banks or customer support

Reporting cybercrime:
* Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
* Contact the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
