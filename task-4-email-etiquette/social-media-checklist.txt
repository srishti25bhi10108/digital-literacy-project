Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student (Part B):
Do’s-
* Think before posting anything online
* Keep your accounts private and use security settings
* Verify information before sharing it
* Maintain a respectful and positive online presence
* Use social media for learning and networking
Don’ts-
* Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or arguments online
* Don’t click on suspicious links or messages
* Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
* Don’t overshare details about your personal life
